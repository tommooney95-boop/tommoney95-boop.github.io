Privacy Policy — Momentum

Last updated: 16 June 2026

Who we are

Momentum ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal habit and goal tracking application developed and operated by Tom Mooney.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the app collects, why we collect it, how it is stored and shared, and the choices and rights you have. It applies to both you (the user) and us (the developer), and is intended to be transparent and honest about how your data is handled.

If you have any questions, contact us at pixelodessey@outlook.com.

The short version

  • You can use Momentum without an account. In that mode, your data stays on your device.
  • If you sign in (email magic link, Google, or Apple), your goals and progress are backed up to our cloud database so you can sync across devices.
  • We do not sell your data, and we do not use third-party advertising or tracking.
  • You can export your data or delete your account and data at any time from within the app.

Where your data is stored

  • On your device: All goals, check-ins, progress, and settings are stored locally so the app works without an internet connection.
  • In the cloud (only if you sign in): When you create an account, your goals and progress are stored in our hosted database provided by Supabase. Access is restricted so that you can only read and write your own data.

If you never sign in, no data leaves your device through the app.

We do not sell your personal information and we do not share it for advertising.

We may also disclose information if required by law, or to protect the rights, safety, or security of users or the public.

Sharing you initiate

The app includes optional share features (for example, sharing a weekly summary or an accountability invite). When you use these, the content is handed to your device's share sheet and sent only to the people and apps you choose. We are not involved in that transmission.

How long we keep data

  • Local data remains on your device until you delete it, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.
  • Cloud data is kept while your account exists. When you delete your account (see below), your goals and progress are removed from our database.

Your rights and choices

  • Use without an account: You can use Momentum locally without signing in.
  • Access / portability: You can export your data as a JSON file from Settings → Data → Export data.
  • Deletion: You can delete your account and local data from Settings → Data → Delete account & local data. This removes your synced goals and progress from our database and signs you out.
  • Manual deletion request: If you cannot use the in-app option, email us at pixelodessey@outlook.com and we will delete your account data.
  • Notifications: You can turn reminders on or off in the app or in your device settings at any time.

Depending on where you live, you may have additional rights (such as under the Singapore Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA), the GDPR, or the CCPA), including the right to access, correct, or delete your data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact pixelodessey@outlook.com.

Security

We rely on industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and access controls that limit cloud data to your own account. No method of storage or transmission is 100% secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your information.

Children's privacy

Momentum is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact pixelodessey@outlook.com and we will delete it.

International users

Your data may be stored and processed in a country other than where you live, including where our service providers operate. By using the app, you understand that your information may be transferred to and processed in those locations.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Significant changes may be communicated in the app or store listing. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
